About the Film

A Visual Storm traces Italian artist Aurelio Caminati’s postwar journey, exploring his avant-garde works and deep connection to Ireland’s rugged Wild Atlantic coast.

Shot over a decade (2012–2022), the film weaves oceanic imagery with Caminati’s evolving artistic vision-capturing storms, solitude, and the creative spark that emerges from nature’s fury.

Featuring Artwork

A curated selection of Caminati’s paintings, installations and sketches, each piece reflecting the interplay of wind, water and human emotion.
